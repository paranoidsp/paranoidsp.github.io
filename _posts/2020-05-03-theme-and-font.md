## Status Quo
The current appearance of this blog is based off of the colors and font from the [Hacker Github Pages theme](https://github.com/pages-themes/hacker).

* * *
## Why is the status quo

I'd like to specify that status is mutable, and will most likely not be quo for most of the lifetime of this blog. I will tend to keep changing the appearance to breathe some fresh air and experiment with my aesthetic sensibilities.

## The Hacker Github theme - 3 March, 2020

### Colors

The green on black is a fond memory of times spent learning, nights debugging and combing through documentation, designing and refactoring. It's a throwback to the time when I thought it was cool to have transparent terminals with green text and tiny fonts. Starting this off with these colors somehow seems fitting, and the l33t h4x0r trope forms an interesting backdrop for what I intend to be a very raw and honest commentary.


### Fonts
I love monospace fonts. I apologise for the trouble it might cause you to read it, but this is again a throwback to those happy times.

For all my monospace work, I use Iosevka. Monaco looks great on the browser, and wormed its way onto this theme. I will eventually try out Iosevka for the blog, and then others.

Other fonts I might try in the future are Noto, Iosevka, Lato. If you have any suggestions about fonts that I might like based on this list, please drop me a mail.
